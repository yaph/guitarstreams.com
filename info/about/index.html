About GuitarStreams

GuitarStreams is a website for guitar and ukulele players featuring video lessons by awesome teachers and musicians. The site was created and is maintained by Ramiro Gómez.

Most of the lessons are song lessons, but there are also technique lessons to become a better player. The songs reflect my personal taste in music, but I think many of them are liked by a lot of guitarists and ukulele players.

Lessons where you play chords feature guitar or ukulele chord charts. Each chord chart links to overview pages with lessons that contain the respective chord. Moreover, song lessons feature a play along video if available. I hope to provide some extra value with these features.

Below all lesson videos you find links to the respective channels as well as links to the teachers' websites and social profiles if they provide this information on their channel pages. I highly recommend to follow them, if you like their style of teaching, because GuitarStreams only features a tiny fraction of the lessons they offer.
