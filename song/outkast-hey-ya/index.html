OutKast - Hey Ya!

"Hey Ya!" was written and by André 3000 for OutKast's 2003 double album Speakerboxxx/The Love Below. The song takes influence from funk, rap and rock music and the music video features a live performance by a band, all 8 members played by André 3000, that mimics the Beatles' 1964 performance on The Ed Sullivan Show.
