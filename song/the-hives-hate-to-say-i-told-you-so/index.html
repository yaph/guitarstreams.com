The Hives - Hate to Say I Told You So

Guitar Ukulele

"Hate to Say I Told You So" is the first single from the Hives' 2nd studio album Veni Vidi Vicious. It was first released as a single on 4 December 2000 and is probably the most iconic song from the Swedish Garage Rock band. Among others the song was featured on the soundtrack for the 2002 film Spider-Man.
