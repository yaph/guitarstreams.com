Manu Chao - La Vida Tómbola

Guitar Ukulele

La Vida Tómbola is a song from Manu Chao about the Argentine football legend Diego Armando Maradona from the album La Radiolina. The song is also featured in the documentary Maradona by Kusturica directed by the Serbian filmmaker Emir Kusturica.
