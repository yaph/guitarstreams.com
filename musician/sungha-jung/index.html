Sungha Jung

Born: September 02, 1996 in Cheongju

Sungha Jung is a South Korean musician who specializes in acoustic fingerstyle guitar. Jung creates acoustic covers and arrangements, typically by ear and/or by watching videos, and has original music, which he plays and uploads online. He is often described as a guitar prodigy, though he prefers to be known as a 'guitarist' rather than a 'prodigy'. His YouTube channel currently has more than 5 million subscribers.

Instruments: Guitar

Genres: Fingerstyle Guitar

Occupations: musician, YouTuber, guitarist
