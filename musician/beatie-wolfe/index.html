Beatie Wolfe

Beatie Wolfe is an Anglo-American singer-songwriter, described as "a pioneering songwriter" known for "seeing music differently" and creating new tangible formats for music, which include the 'World's First 3D Interactive Album App' for her debut album 8ight, a musical jacket for her second album Montagu Square and the world's first live 360 AR stream for her third album Raw Space. These musical innovations have gained Wolfe critical acclaim with Highsnobiety naming Wolfe as one of '10 Ways Music Will Change in 2017' and Wired (magazine) featuring Wolfe as one of 22 changing the world.

Genres: indie rock, folk music, lo-fi music
Instruments: guitar, piano, voice
Influenced by: Elliott Smith, Leonard Cohen, John Lennon, Blaze Foley, The Beatles
Occupations: singer, singer-songwriter, guitarist, Innovator, artist
