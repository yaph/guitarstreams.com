Rick Derringer

Born: August 05, 1947 in Fort Recovery

Rick Derringer is an American guitarist, vocalist, Grammy Award-winning producer and writer of multiple hit songs. He came to prominence in the 1960s as founding member of his band, the McCoys. At that time, they were brought to New York City to record what became the number-one hit song, 'Hang On Sloopy'. The McCoys at seven more songs to chart in the Top 100, including their versions of 'Come on, Let's Go' and 'Fever.'
