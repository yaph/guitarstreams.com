7 Modern Musical Modes Cheat Sheet

This cheat sheet shows the 7 modern musical modes Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Aeolian, Locrian, and for each of them the tonic relative to the major scale, the step sequence and an example whole note scale. The scales make clear how these modes work and show that music theory does not have to be hard to understand. Knowing how modes work and more importantly how to use them is helpful for writing your own music. Get this design on one of the available products to help you memorize the modern modes.
