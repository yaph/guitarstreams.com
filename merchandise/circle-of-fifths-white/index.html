Description

The circle of fifths is a music theoretical tool to visualize the relationships between the 12 notes of the chromatic scale, their key signatures and the associated major and minor keys. This design shows the Circle of Fifths with the root notes of the major scales in the outer and the roots of the corresponding relative minor scales in the inner circle.
