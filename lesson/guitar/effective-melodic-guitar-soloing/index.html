Effective Melodic Guitar Soloing Tips from Steve Stine

In this lesson Steve Stine teaches how to make your guitar soloing sound more emotional and melodic. It is important to see how chords in a progression are connected to the scale you use as the base for your soloing, in his example the major pentatonic scale.

To discover notes you can add, it is immensely helpful to know movable chord shapes, which allows you to pick notes in close proximity to the position of your scale on the fretboard. Visualizing these connections and putting them into practice will benefit your soloing and your fretboard knowledge. Go ahead, give it a try and have fun with your guitar.
