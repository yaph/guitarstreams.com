Otis Redding - (Sittin' On) Dock Of The Bay

In this guitar lesson Justin Sandercoe teaches how to play the song The Dock of the Bay by Otis Redding. Justin teaches the song using open chords, but is also well-suited for using barre chords.

Play Along Song

The official music video for (Sittin' On) The Dock Of The Bay by Otis Redding featuring clips and photos of Otis Redding in the prime of his musical career. This classic RnB tune was co-written by Otis Redding and guitarist Steve Cropper and recorded twice by Redding in 1967, once just days before he died in a plane crash.

The record was released in 1968 on Stax Records' Volt label becoming the first posthumous single to top the charts in the US. It reached number 3 on the UK Singles Chart.
