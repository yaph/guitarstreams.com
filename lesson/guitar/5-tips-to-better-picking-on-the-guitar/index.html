5 Tips To Better Picking On The Guitar

In this lesson Nate Savage gives 5 tips for better picking on the guitar. A pretty subjective topic, thus he shows things that worked for him over the years, but they are certainly valuable tips, even if you do things differently. In the end one has to find our for oneself what works best.
