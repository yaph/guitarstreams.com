Setting Up A Second Hand Guitar

In this video Andrew and Nate from Guitareo show how you can set up a used guitar and give tips on what to ask the seller and look for when picking it up.

They use a $85 Squier Affinity Stratocaster bought on Craigslist to demonstrate how to clean the guitar and make adjustments to the neck, nut and bridge to make it sound and play nicely. At the end of the video you can hear how it sounds compared to a a $1000 Fender American Stratocaster. They did a good job.

A helpful video for the DIY folks among you whether you are low on budget or just love to tinker with your guitar.
