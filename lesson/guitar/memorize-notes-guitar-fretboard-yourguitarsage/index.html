How to Memorize the Notes on the Guitar Fretboard

Erich Andreas links: Website • Youtube • Facebook • Twitter

Erich Andreas breaks down the basic things you need to know to memorize the entire fretboard of a guitar:

  • The notes when playing the 6 strings open: E A D G B E (Eddie Ate Dynamite Good Bye Eddie)
  • The musical alphabet A B C D E F G
  • All notes except for B and E have a sharp/flat.

When I first watched this video I had a moment of enlightenment with regards to the order of the notes. In the German naming system, I was taught, A is followed by H (B in English) and B corresponds to B flat in English. So the alphabetical order is messed up in German, whereas using the English naming convention all notes are in alphabetical order, which makes it a lot more accessible.
