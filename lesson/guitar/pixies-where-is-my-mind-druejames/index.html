Pixies - Where Is My Mind

Learn to play Where Is My Mind by Pixies with this barre chord guitar lesson by Drue James. Drue teaches the intro, the verse and the chorus focusing on the rhythm guitar.

Play Along Song

"Where Is My Mind?" is a song by the American alternative rock band Pixies. It's the 7th track on their 1988 debut album Surfer Rosa and was written by singer and guitarist Black Francis, inspired by his experiences while scuba diving in the Caribbean. The song is used on several TV series and films, most notably in the final scene and ending credits of the 1999 film Fight Club.
