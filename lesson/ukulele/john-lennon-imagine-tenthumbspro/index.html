John Lennon - Imagine

Tyler Austenfeld teaches his audience how to play the peace movement anthem Imagine by John Lennon on the Ukulele.

Play Along Song

Imagine was written and originally performed by John Lennon. It was released on 11 October 1971 and became the best-selling single of his solo career. The lyrics encourage the listener to imagine a world at peace. Lennon and Yoko Ono co-produced the song and album of the same name with Phil Spector.
