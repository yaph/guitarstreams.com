Pixies - Where Is My Mind

In this lesson Tyler Austenfeld teaches the alternative rock classic Where Is My Mind by the Pixies. He shows how to play the intro melody and the 2 chord progressions originally played on the guitar. Most of the chords he uses are barre chords. While you can replace some of them with easier to play open chords, they really go well together and it sounds closer to the original song if you ask me and I guess Tyler thinks so too.

Play Along Song

"Where Is My Mind?" is a song by the American alternative rock band Pixies. It's the 7th track on their 1988 debut album Surfer Rosa and was written by singer and guitarist Black Francis, inspired by his experiences while scuba diving in the Caribbean.

The song is used on several TV series and films, most notably in the final scene and ending credits of the 1999 film Fight Club.
