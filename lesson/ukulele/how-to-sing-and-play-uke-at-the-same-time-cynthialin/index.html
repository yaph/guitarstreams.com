How to Sing and Play Uke at the Same Time

Guitarhabits links: Website • Youtube • Facebook • Twitter • Patreon

A tutorial by Cynthia Lin on how to sing and play the ukulele at the same time using Sam Cooke's Stand By Me as a practice song. Cynthia recommends breaking down your practice into the following four steps:

  1. Just sing (without playing)
  2. Sing and airstrum
  3. Speak the words and strum
  4. Sing and strum super slow
