Sympathy for the Devil - The Rolling Stones

Tyler Austenfeld teaches how to play Sympathy for the Devil by The Rolling Stones on the Ukulele. He gives a breakdown of the 4 chords in this song and shows how to strum with and without muting the strings.

Play Along Song

"Sympathy for the Devil" is a song by The Rolling Stones, written by Mick Jagger and Keith Richards. The song is an ironic homage to The Devil, written in the first-person narrative from his point of view, recounting atrocities committed throughout the history of humanity. It is performed in a rock arrangement with a samba rhythm.

"Sympathy for the Devil" first appeared as the opening track on the Stones' 1968 album Beggars Banquet.
