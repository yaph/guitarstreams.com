Creep - Radiohead

TheUkuleleTeacher links: Youtube • Facebook • Twitter • Patreon

The Ukulele Teacher shows how to play the popular song Creep by Radiohead on the Ukulele. It is just 4 chords repeated throughout the whole song. Except for the G chord, he plays all chords as bar chords.

"Creep" is a song by the English alternative rock band Radiohead, who released it as their debut single in 1992. It later appeared on Radiohead's first album, Pablo Honey (1993). The initial release of Creep was not a chart success, but with its re-release in 1993, it became a worldwide hit.
