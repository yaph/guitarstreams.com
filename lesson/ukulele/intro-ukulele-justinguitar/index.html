Introduction to the Ukulele

Justin Sandercoe links: Website • Youtube • Facebook • Twitter

This is Justin "Guitar" Sandercoe's introductory Ukulele lesson. He shows how to tune a Ukulele and gives tips on holding it, though you really need to figure out yourself what's best for you.

Justin also teaches four basic cords C, G, Am, and F, a.k.a. the '1564' chord progression, that can be used to play a lot of songs on the Ukulele.
