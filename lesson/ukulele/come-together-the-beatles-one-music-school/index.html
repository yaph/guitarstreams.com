The Beatles - Come Together

Katie DeNure links: Website • Youtube • Twitter

In this lesson Katie DeNure from One Music School teaches the famous Beatles tune Come Together. Katie breaks down the intro riff, which includes a hammer on, slide and vibrato, the chord progressions of the verse and chorus and two strumming patterns you can use.

The tutorial part is followed by a full play-through of the song by Katie. She sings with her beautiful voice and plays the ukulele with great technique using a pick, which works very well for this song.
